What is GB WhatsApp?

GB WhatsApp is a modified version of the popular messaging app WhatsApp, offering users additional features and customization options that are not available in the official app. If you want to visit website of GB Whatsapp click here

Key Features of GB WhatsApp:

  • Customization Options: GB WhatsApp allows users to customize the appearance of the app, including themes, fonts, and icons.
  • Privacy Enhancements: Features like hiding your online status, blue ticks, and typing indicators. You can also view deleted messages and media.
  • Increased File Sharing Limits: GB WhatsApp allows sending larger files, such as videos up to 50MB, compared to the standard 16MB limit on WhatsApp.
  • Multiple Accounts: Users can run multiple WhatsApp accounts on a single device, a feature not natively available on WhatsApp.
